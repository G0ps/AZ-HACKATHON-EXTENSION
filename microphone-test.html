Troubleshooting:

  1. Make sure your microphone is properly connected to your computer.
  2. When prompted, click "Allow" to give this page permission to use your microphone.
  3. If you accidentally denied permission, click the lock/info icon in the address bar and change the microphone setting to "Allow".
  4. Try refreshing the page after granting permission.
  5. Check your system settings to ensure your microphone is set as the default input device.
